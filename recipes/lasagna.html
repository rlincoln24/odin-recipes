Lasagna Receipe

Description

This is Robert's favorite lasagna recipe he learned from his grandmother. This Lasagna was painstakingly taught
to Robert by his grandmother and he still makes it the same way she taught him to make it when he was a kid.

I hope you enjoy this recipe!!


Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1 lb ground veal
  • 3 16 oz cans of Whole Peeled San Marzano Tomatoes
  • 3 Large heads of Garlic, peeled and minced
  • 5 Bay Leaves
  • 2 Large Sweet Onions, diced
  • 2 tbsp Fresh oregano, chopped finely
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Basil, chopped finely
  • 1 Bottle of Red Wine
  • 2 16 oz containers of your favorite Ricotta Cheese
  • 2 lbs of Whole Milk Mozzarella
  • 2 Whole Eggs
  • 1 oz parsley
  • 2 Packages of Lasagna Noodles, cooked per instructions on container


Bolognese Steps

  1. Heat to Medium, Add onions and sweat until soft and translucent then remove and set aside.
  2. Increase heat to high for 5 minutes
  3. Add the Ground Beef and sear for 2-3 minutes or until deeply caramlized
  4. Remove the ground beef and repeat with the Ground Pork and Veal.
  5. Add the Ground Beef, Pork and Veal to the Dutch Oven and continue cooking until well browned
  6. Remove meat and deglaze pan with Red Wine. Be sure to scrape all or as much of the bits from the bottom of the pan.
  7. Add all 3 cans of Tomatoes and hand crush each tomato before adding it to the Dutch Oven.
  8. Readd Beef, Port and Veal to the Dutch Oven
  9. Add Garlic, Bay Leaves, Oregano, Basil
  10. Add 1 Cup of Red Wine
  11. Simmer on low for 4 hours stirring regularly

Cheese mixture steps

  1. Add Ricotta to mixing bowl
  2. Add 1 cup shredded Mozzarella
  3. Add Parsley
  4. Add 2 egg yolks only, no whites
  5. Mix until combined well

Assembly Steps

  1. Scoop a small amount of Bolognese and spread on the bottom of a large baking dish atleast 3-4" deep
  2. Add layer of Lasagna Noodles
  3. Add layer of Bolognese
  4. Add layer of lasagna noodles
  5. Add Layer of cheese mixture
  6. Repeat Steps 2-4, 2-3 more times
  7. Add thick layer of shredded Mozzarella on top
  8. Cover with Aluminum Foil (tent to ensure foil does not make contact with cheese) and bake for 1 hour at 375 degrees
  9. Turn oven off, Turn Broiler on and remove aluminum foil
  10. Brown Cheese until golden brown
  11. Remove from Oven and let cool for 30 minutes
  12. Enjoy!!
