Tomato Soup Recipe

Description

Call me old fashioned but I love Cambells Tomato soup and I'd love to share how I prepare it with you.

This is how my grandmother prepared Tomato soup for me when I was a kid and is how i've prepared it for my kids as well. I hope you enjoy it!!

I always enjoy this recipe with a classic grilled cheese sandwich as well.


Ingredients

  • 1 Large Can of Cambells Tomato Soup
  • 2% Milk equal to the amount of Tomato soup

Steps

  1. In a medium Sauce Pan add the Tomato Soup from the can.
  2. Fill the empty can with 2% milk and using a whisk mix around inside to get as much of the soup mix out of the can.
  3. Empty the milk into the pot with the Tomato Soup
  4. On low heat, stir consantly until tomato soup is to your desired soup temparature.
  5. Pour into a large bowl and enjoy!!
