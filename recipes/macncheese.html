Macaroni and Cheese


Description

This is my favorite Macaroni and Cheese recipe.


Ingredients

  • 1 box macroni, cooked per instructions on box
  • 2 cups finely shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups finely shredded medium cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp cracked black pepper
  • 2 tbsp Salt

Steps

  1. Grease a large 13x9 baking dish with butter
  2. Add Macaroni, Medium Cheddar Cheese, Heavy Cream, Onion Powder, Pepper and Salt
  3. Mix until well combined
  4. Cover with Aluminum foil and bake in a 350 degree oven for 45 - 60 Minutes
  5. Remove Foil and add Sharp Cheddar cheese on top
  6. Set broiler to High and broil until cheese is golden brown
  7. Remove from oven and let cool for 30 minutes before serving
